In addition, because ADLumin-5 is dual-functional--enabling both photodegradation and in vivo imaging of misfolded protein changes--it can be considered a photo-theranostic agent for neurodegenerative diseases, representing a novel approach to drug discovery for neurodegenerative diseases.
